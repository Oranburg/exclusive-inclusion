Exclusive Inclusion

Why institutions committed to “inclusion” produce identity-based exclusion

Seth C. Oranburg · SSRN Working Paper

Read the Paper View on SSRN Explore Learning Resources

Abstract

This paper examines the paradox at the heart of contemporary diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) programs: the very institutions most committed to “inclusion” systematically produce identity-based exclusion. Drawing on organizational theory, administrative law, and critical analysis, the paper explains why facially neutral inclusion commitments generate discriminatory outcomes—and proposes a framework for understanding and addressing the structural causes of this paradox.

Oranburg, Seth C., Exclusive Inclusion (2025). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=6423378
