Structural Reconstruction
The Three Reforms
⚖️
Reform 1: Stakeholder Standing
Enrolled students and current faculty may bring derivative suits enforcing existing fiduciary duties against nonprofit educational boards.
Option-Foreclosing: legislatively durable
📜
Reform 2: Mission Specificity
Mission statements must meet an operational specificity standard — encoding measurable equal-treatment commitments, triggering all six Buckles obedience norms.
Option-Foreclosing: fiduciary breach to depart
🏛️
Reform 3: Operational Test
IRS conditions 501(c)(3) status on demonstrated governance structures preventing systematic identity-based exclusion; modeled on hospital community benefit standard (I.R.C. § 501(r)).
Option-Foreclosing: tax-exemption condition
🔗
Mutual Reinforcement
Standing without standards is empty. Standards without standing are unenforceable. External accountability without internal mechanisms can be satisfied on paper. Together: overlapping accountability redundancy.
Systemic: all three required
✓
Governance Void?
All three reforms directly target the three architectural features of the governance void: absent standing, hollow duty, self-perpetuating board.
